The problem

Every security category has a benchmark. AI doesn't.

Cloud security has CIS benchmarks. AppSec has OWASP. Identity has zero-trust frameworks. AI security has... manual red-teaming once a quarter, if you're lucky.

Your LLMs are in production. Users are talking to them right now. How confident are you that they won't leak your system prompt, bypass your guardrails, or get manipulated into harmful outputs?

What we do

Continuous adversarial testing for your AI stack

🎯

8 Attack Categories

Direct extraction, prompt chaining, roleplay exploits, transformation abuse, authority escalation, obfuscation, ambiguity probing, and persistence attacks.

🤖

AI-Powered Scoring

LLM-as-judge evaluates each response semantically — catches leaks that regex can't. Scores on a 0–4 leak scale with confidence ratings.

📊

Resilience Score

Composite score across 5 dimensions: injection resistance, leakage prevention, guardrail accuracy, ambiguity handling, transformation resistance.

🔒

Zero Data Exfiltration

On-prem scan agent runs inside your network. Your system prompts, API keys, and model responses never leave your environment. We only see the score.

🔄

Adaptive Red Team

AI agent that reads responses, adapts strategy, and keeps probing until it succeeds or exhausts its budget. Stops early if the model is resistant.

📈

Track Over Time

Run scans weekly or on every deployment. Track your resilience score over time. Get alerted when it drops below your threshold.

How it works

1
